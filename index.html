Transitions

W3Schools

To animate something in CSS, we need to specify the property and the timespan

original: width 100px, transition: 2s

hover: width 300px

transition can support different times for specific properties at once

transition timing can be configured with transition-timing-function.

transition can be delayed with transition-delay. This example: 2 seconds

transition can have a value of transform(time). A list of transforms can be found here
